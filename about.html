Our Journey Through Time

2015

The Beginning

Started with just 3 sewing machines in a 500 sq ft workshop. Our first product line featured 5 basic t-shirt designs that quickly gained attention for their exceptional fabric quality.

2017

First Major Innovation

Developed our proprietary fabric blend that combined breathability with durability. This innovation became our signature and set new standards in the industry.

2019

National Expansion

Opened our second manufacturing facility and expanded distribution to all major cities across India. Partnered with 50+ custom printing businesses nationwide.

2022

Sustainable Transformation

Implemented eco-friendly manufacturing processes and introduced our first line of organic cotton products. Reduced water consumption by 40% across operations.

2024

Digital Revolution

Launched our complete digital ecosystem, serving thousands of businesses across India with real-time inventory, AI-powered recommendations, and seamless logistics.

Our Journey in Apparel

From humble beginnings to a trusted national B2B wholesale manufacturer of plain t-shirts, we've been crafting quality apparel with passion and precision since 2023.

Our Journey Through Time

2023

The Beginning

Started with just 3 sewing machines in a 100 sq ft workshop. Our first product line featured 2 basic t-shirt designs that quickly gained attention for their exceptional fabric quality.

2024

First Major Innovation

Developed our proprietary fabric blend that combined breathability with durability. This innovation became our signature and set new industry standards.

2025

National Expansion

Opened our second manufacturing facility and expanded distribution to all major cities across India. Partnered with 25+ custom printing businesses nationwide.

2025

Sustainable Transformation

Implemented eco-friendly manufacturing processes and introduced our first line of organic cotton products. Reduced water consumption by 40% across operations.

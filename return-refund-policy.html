Return & Refund Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

Thank you for shopping with MyBrand. Your satisfaction is important to us.

1. Order Cancellation Rights

You may cancel your order within 7 days from the date you receive the goods.

To cancel an order:

  1. Send us a clear cancellation request via WhatsApp: +91-9336695049
  2. Include your order number and reason for cancellation

Refund for Cancelled Orders: We will reimburse 80% of the product value within 2 business days of receiving the returned goods.

Please Note:

  • Taxes and shipping charges are non-refundable
  • Goods purchased during clearance sales cannot be refunded
  • Personalized/customized items cannot be cancelled

2. Conditions for Returns

To be eligible for a return, your item must meet the following conditions:

Eligibility Requirements:

  • The goods were purchased within the last 7 days
  • The goods are in their original packaging with all tags attached
  • The goods are unused, unwashed, and undamaged
  • You have the original receipt or proof of purchase
Product Type Return Eligibility Special Conditions
Regular T-Shirts ✅ Eligible Must have original tags
Custom Printed Items ❌ Not Eligible Made to specifications
Clearance Sale Items ❌ Not Eligible Final sale
Damaged/Defective Items ✅ Eligible Report within 24 hours

Goods That Cannot Be Returned:

  1. Goods made to your specifications or clearly personalized
  2. Goods unsuitable for return due to health/hygiene reasons that were unsealed after delivery
  3. Goods that are, according to their nature, inseparably mixed with other items after delivery
  4. Intimate apparel or undergarments for hygiene reasons

We reserve the right to refuse returns of any merchandise that does not meet the above conditions.

Step-by-Step Return Process:

  1. Contact Us: Send return request via WhatsApp with order details and reason
  2. Get Approval: Wait for our return authorization (RMA number)
  3. Pack Securely: Place items in original packaging with all tags
  4. Ship to Us: Send to our address (provided below) using trackable service
  5. Track & Wait: Monitor your shipment and wait for processing

5. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective product:

  1. Report Immediately: Contact us within 24 hours of delivery
  2. Provide Evidence: Send clear photos/videos of the damage
  3. Do Not Dispose: Keep all packaging and tags
  4. Wait for Instructions: We'll guide you on the next steps

For damaged/defective items verified by our team, we will either:

  • Provide a full refund (100% of product value)
  • Send a replacement at no additional cost
  • Cover return shipping costs
